# SPYME Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: May 2026**

SPYME is built around one principle: **we don't store your videos, ever.**

## What we collect

| Data | Stored where | Why |
|------|--------------|-----|
| Email + name | Our database (Postgres, US/EU region) | Account login |
| Phone number (optional) | Our database | Fire-emergency SMS only |
| Device names ("Bedroom Laptop") | Our database | Multi-device dashboard |
| Event log (timestamp + type, e.g. "person at 3:42pm") | Our database | Show your alert history |
| **Video clips** | **NEVER on our servers.** Stays on your laptop, your phone, or your own Google Drive. | Privacy by design |
| **Live video** | **NEVER on our servers.** P2P WebRTC stream — laptop talks straight to your phone. | Privacy by design |
| **Audio** | **NEVER recorded.** | Privacy by design |

## What we DON'T do

- We do not sell your data.
- We do not share with advertisers.
- We do not share with police unless served a valid US court order.
- We do not analyze your video on our servers (the AI runs on your laptop).
- We do not store fingerprints, faces, or biometric data anywhere except on your laptop (face recognition is local-only).

## Your rights

- Export everything we have about you: in-app, anytime.
- Delete your account: in-app, instant. Wipes all metadata in 24 hours.
- GDPR / CCPA / India DPDPA compliant.

## Data location

You choose your region at signup: **US East (Virginia)**, **EU (Frankfurt)**, or **Asia (Singapore)**. Data never leaves your chosen region.

## Third parties we use

| Service | What for | What they see |
|---------|----------|---------------|
| Google OAuth | "Continue with Google" | Your Google email + profile only (if you choose Google sign-in) |
| Twilio | Fire-emergency SMS | Your phone number + alert text (only when fire detected) |
| Firebase Cloud Messaging | Push notifications | Notification title/body only (no video) |
| Stripe | Subscription billing | Payment details (we never see your card) |

## Camera & microphone permissions

- Camera access is requested by your laptop's OS — you must approve.
- A persistent "SPYME monitoring" indicator is always visible while armed (we never hide it).
- The microphone is **never** activated.
